About

Capturing the Soul
of Yemen

Through my lens, I seek the poetry hidden in everyday moments — the way light falls on ancient stone, the geometry of shadows in Sana'a's old city, the quiet dignity in a stranger's gaze. Every photograph is a conversation between light and memory.

Based between Sana'a and the world, I document stories that linger in the space between tradition and tomorrow. My work is an homage to the landscapes, faces, and textures that define the Yemeni spirit — resilient, beautiful, and endlessly inspiring.
